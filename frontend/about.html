About PlacementorAI

Helping students find their dream jobs and track applications efficiently. Our mission is to empower students with tools that make job hunting smarter, easier, and faster.

Our Mission

To simplify the job search and placement process for students by providing a smart, intuitive platform that connects them with opportunities matching their skills and ambitions.

Our Vision

We envision a world where every student can easily discover the right career path, showcase their talents, and achieve their professional dreams.

Smart Job Matching

Application Tracking

Profile Analytics

Know how complete your profile is and what you can improve to increase chances.
